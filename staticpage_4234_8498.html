4/17 - It’s a Small World 50th anniversary



This beautiful limited edition pin celebrates the 50th anniversary of the “It’s a Small World” attraction. The attraction was first introduced at the 1964 World Fair. This unique hinged pin opens to reveal the iconic buildings from the “It’s a Small World” attraction. The top hinge features glitter fill and stained glass. The fair was held in Queens, New York from April 22 to October 18, 1964 and then again from April 21 to October 17, 1965 for a total of 260 days. More than 51 million people attended the fair.

 It’s a Small World was created for the 1964 New York World’s Fair’s UNICEF pavilion. Spectators journeyed past France’s Eiffel Tower, a Dutch windmill, and India’s Taj Mahal. The animated children, animals, and birds sang and danced to the Sherman Brothers song called “It’s a Small World.”
