Jun. 28th - Orlando, FL

This MousePinTrading.com Pin & Vinylmation Trading Day is scheduled for Saturday, June 28th, 2014 from 9am to 5pm at the Holiday Inn - Lake Buena Vista Downtown, in Orlando, FL; just minutes from Downtown Disney.

We're here to bring together the Disney Pin and Vinylmation community for a fun and exciting trading event. There will be people trading both Disney Pins and Vinylmations throughout the day. You'll also have the opportunity to will prizes in our raffle and also a chance at either of our Pin Trading Boards or Vinylmation Trading Box.

We encourage all fans, traders, and collectors to join us for an amazing day of trading and fun! Whether you've been trading for years or are new to the hobby, then this is the perfect event for you to attend.

Just like in past months, THERE WILL BE NO BLATANT SELLING AT THIS EVENT! If a trader wishes to allow people to make cash offers, they will have a special sign posted at their table. Only those tables that have this sign are willing to accept cash offers for their pins/vinylmations. All other tables are STRICTLY Trade ONLY!. Any offers to purchase pins or vinylmations are solely at the discretion of the purchaser. We donâ€™t want anyone to feel pressured to make a purchase offer instead of a trade.

If you would like to join us for this fun-filled day of trading, please make sure to join the "Disney Pin Collector's Society" Facebook Group, that's where we post all the info.  There will be 6 foot tables available for $20 for the day, or if you just want to come over and walk around and trade, the cost is $5 per couple/family.  All money collected just goes to cover the costs of renting the space and putting on the event.  Additionally, the hotel charges $2 per car for self-parking.

We look forward to you joining us for a fun day of trading, games, and friends.
