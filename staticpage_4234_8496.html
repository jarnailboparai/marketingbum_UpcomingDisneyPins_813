4/10 - Piece of Disney History


This pin features the Old Hag from the Snow White’s Scary Adventure attraction at the Disneyland® Resort. This pin features pin-on-pin, marble fill, and 3D sculpted PVC embellishments. This pin contains a piece of the Old Hag’s black hood.
